Workflow to Produce Assets Consistently

Based on our internal best practices, these four steps help teams ship reliable AI visuals while reducing guesswork and revision cycles.

Step 1

Clarify requirements

Map the delivery channel, aspect ratio, brand keywords, and reference palette. Store the essentials in prompt notes so the team stays aligned.

Step 2

Pick model and parameters

Choose FLUX for premium output, Turbo for speed, or Kontext for image-to-image. Set aspect ratio, variation count, and negative prompts to match the channel.

Step 3

Iterate prompts

Generate a draft, inspect composition and lighting, then adjust keywords. Use prompt history to compare versions and lock in the strongest option.

Step 4

Export and add voice

Upscale and export the hero frame as PNG/JPG. Switch to the voice page to create narration with the same script for a full campaign package.
